How to Play: Hockey Heroes

Objective:
Build your ultimate NHL dream team deck, outsmart your opponent, and win rounds by choosing the best player stats in head-to-head matchups!

Setup:
- Each player has a deck of NHL player cards.
- Each card includes key stats (e.g., Goals, Assists, Hits, Saves, Plus/Minus, etc.).
- Shuffle your deck and draw a starting hand (e.g., 5 cards – customizable based on your rules).

Gameplay:
1. Face-Off Turn: Players take turns being the Stat Caller. The Stat Caller picks one stat from their chosen card (e.g., "Goals") for the round’s battle.
2. Stat Showdown: Both players reveal their selected cards. Compare the chosen stat.
  - The player with the higher stat wins the round and scores a point (or takes both cards, depending on your scoring system).
3. Next Round: Switch who calls the stat each round and keep the action going!
4. Deck Management: After each round, players can draw a new card from their deck to replenish their hand (optional rule: manage a limited hand to add strategy).
5. Trade & Build: Between games, trade cards with friends to strengthen your team and strategize for upcoming battles.

Winning the Game:
- First to a set number of points (e.g., 10) wins!
- Or play until one player runs out of cards—the player with the most points wins.

Pro Tips:
- Know your stats! Smart stat choices can swing the game.
- Build a balanced deck—offensive firepower, defensive anchors, elite goalies—every player counts.
- Strategize trades to fill gaps and counter your rivals.
